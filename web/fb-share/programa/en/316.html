Restoration of the legality of border checkpoints: respect for human rights on the southern border

We will put an immediate end to the illegal expulsion of people in the border areas, which violates international laws. To do this, we will repeal the relevant articles of the Statutory Law 4/2015, 30th March, for the protection of citizen security.

Also, we will end the human rights violations that occur on the southern border. To do this, we will remove the harmful elements placed at border perimeter of Ceuta and Melilla and promote the adoption of a protocol of the State Security Forces on this southern border. In addition, we will review the bilateral treaty with Morocco, to ensure strict compliance with the principle of no enforced return.
